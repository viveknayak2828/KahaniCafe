Good Food Good Vibe

A cafe that offers great food and juice is the perfect spot to enjoy a delicious meal or a refreshing drink. The menu usually includes a variety of fresh, healthy options, such as salads, sandwiches,as well as heartier dishes like burgers and pasta. The ingredients are often locally sourced and organic, ensuring that each dish is full of flavor and nutrition.

In addition to the food, a great cafe will also have a selection of fresh juices and smoothies, made with seasonal fruits and vegetables. These drinks are a great way to stay hydrated while also getting a boost of vitamins and minerals.The atmosphere of a great cafe is usually warm and welcoming, with comfortable seating areas where customers can relax and enjoy their meals.

Kahani Cafe

As young kids when we were taught ‘Living things need oxygen to survive’, we believed only oxygen was more than enough for us to survive! But only after we started to grow, cross the adolescent age and got to evolve, life taught us there were other things that made us not lose hope even during our worst time, that instilled confidence, which brought smiles, tears on our face!

Yes, it's Food and Films that played a pivotal role in our life, which connected both of us & made us fall in love with each other. The powerful storytelling in Mani Ratnam films or the depiction of each frame in scenes shot by P C Sreeram or Santosh Sivan, the performance of legendary actors like Rajkumar, Kamal Hassan and the indelible style of filmmaking of Puttanna Kanagal to Pa Ranjith. The dialogue-heavy melodramatic performances of our yesteryear stars to the lyrics penned by Chi Udayashankar to Jayant Kaikini or the soul stirring music of Hamsalekha, A R Rahman or Ilayajara, all these things made us fall in love to the craft of filmmaking. Films taught us to love, hate, laugh, cry, values and above all it doubled our hope, passion and faith in life. Similarly it was Food which accompanied us in this journey. Food isn't just our daily sustenance; it truly does have a more profound meaning in our lives. Food has been our medicine and medicine to us is the food, nothing else. Being both film and food buffs, we came out with this unique concept of amalgamation of food and film. A one-of-its kind café in Namma Mysuru, which serves not just food but creates a platform to discuss cinema, art and music. Being passionate about food and film, we allowed our passion to become our purpose, and today it has become our profession. Only passions, great passions can elevate the soul to great things, and our passion as brought us here, thus KaHaNi café. There is no end. There is no beginning. There is only the infinite passion of life.. This is the ‘Kahani’ behind the ‘KaHaNi’ café.
